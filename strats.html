The Rock-Paper-Scissors meta

The meta puts most units in three different catagories. tough and tanky units that have little movment but high defence are rocks(E.G. Warriors), Sissors are units that can attack from a distance and deal decent damage but a low defence and health(E.G. Archer), and Paper units, which have a large movment range, medium to high attack and low defence(E.G. Rider).

Different unit combonations

Do you know what's better than units on their own?

That's right, units that use their abilities with other units that complement them.

When you want to use to different types of units together, it's best to have supporting units and a main attacking unit, or a unit that, when it sieges a city, it will hold that siege.

This means that you should always have a "Rock" unit on the frontlines and a "Paper" or "Scissors" on the backline.
